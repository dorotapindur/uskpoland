Free workshops information

There is a limit of 20 participants for each workshop. Registration is closed, all lessons are fully booked. Below you can find information about materials required during workshops - please read carefully and bring all necessary tools.

Materials

Some of the materials will be provided by sponsors. We will use: paper for watercolor or mixed techniques with a minimum weight of 200 g, waterbrush, watercolors in basic colors, white Posca marker 0.7 mm.

Please bring with you:

  • colored pencils (turquoise or light blue, light red, yellow, dark blue or purple)
  • HB pencil (optionally)
  • favorite brushes and a water container
