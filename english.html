Urban Sketching Festival

Urban Sketchers from Poland and Europe, let’s unite! We invite you to the first international Urban Sketching Festival in Świdnica - from 11 to 14 August 2022 this beautiful city in Lower Silesia, Poland, will be filled with amateurs of sketching on location. Drawing meetings among monuments, open-air workshops, exhibition, lectures, art materials fair are just some of the attractions; there will also be an opportunity to meet interesting people and join the international community admiring the world with a sketchbook in hand. Participation in the festival is completely free and you can take part in any number of events. Due to the limited number of participants, there is a registration for free open-air workshops that will be conducted by Justyna Wojnowska, Adam Michen, Krzysztof Ludwin and Jan Vitek from Prague.

Organizers: Urban Sketchers Poland, Świdnica Cultural Center and the international organization Urban Sketchers.

Urban Sketchers Poland

WHO WE ARE? We are a group promoting sketching on location in urban spaces, bringing together drawing enthusiasts of all skill levels. Urban Sketchers is a grassroots initiative that is implemented around the world, and its cradle is Seattle.

WHAT WE DO? We sketch the world around us using all available techniques, basing on direct observation and the experience of being in a given place. We publish our works on the Internet and support each other in creative development, creating a unique international community.

FOR WHOM? Our drawing meetings are for everyone, regardless of age, opinions and skills - all you need is to like to draw (or paint). We want to show the world seen through our eyes, one sketch at a time.

We sketch in the spirit of our manifesto.

  • We draw on location, indoors or out, capturing what we see from direct observation.
  • Our drawings tell the story of our surroundings, the places we live, and where we travel.
  • Our drawings are a record of time and place.
  • We are truthful to the scenes we witness.
  • We use any kind of media and cherish our individual styles.
  • We support each other and draw together.
  • We share our drawings online.
  • We show the world, one drawing at a time.

Urban Sketchers is a non-profit organization and it's mission is to raise the artistic, storytelling, and educational value of on-location drawing, promoting its practice and connecting people worldwide who draw on location where they live and travel.

Urban Sketchers Poland is a local chapter of this organization, and its goal is to spread the above mentioned values and ideas in Poland.
