Become a Volunteer

VOLUNTEERS WANTED! The mission of Urban Sketchers, as a non-profit organization, is to promote artistic, historical, and educational values derived from sketching urban locations and connecting people worldwide who draw in the places where they live and travel.

Why is it worth becoming a volunteer at the Urban Sketchers Symposium? Urban Sketchers is entirely managed by volunteers and the annual Symposium is our flagship event where people from all over the world gather to meet, learn and sketch together. Volunteering is one of the best ways to support and contribute to the Symposium. This event inspires our international community to grow and helps promote the movement to new people.

Volunteering is one of the best ways to support and get involved with the Symposium. As a volunteer, you will experience the Symposium in a different way, meet new friends, help organize a world-class event, and enjoy the company of people who love what you love!

What else will you gain by becoming a volunteer:

  • direct contact with organizers and instructors
  • a behind-the-scenes look at organizing such a large event
  • a special volunteer package

Detailed information, scope of responsibilities and benefits can be found in the application form: Apply to volunteer. Application deadline: March 8, 2025.

Urban Sketchers Poland

WHO WE ARE? We are a group promoting sketching on location in urban spaces, bringing together drawing enthusiasts of all skill levels. Urban Sketchers is a grassroots initiative that is implemented around the world, and its cradle is Seattle.

WHAT WE DO? We sketch the world around us using all available techniques, based on direct observation and the experience of being in a given place. We publish our works on the Internet and support each other in creative development, creating a unique international community.

FOR WHOM? Our drawing meetups are for everyone, regardless of age, opinions and skills - all you need is to like to draw (or paint). We want to show the world seen through our eyes, one sketch at a time.

We sketch in the spirit of our manifesto.

  • We draw on location, indoors or out, capturing what we see from direct observation.
  • Our drawings tell the story of our surroundings, the places we live, and where we travel.
  • Our drawings are a record of time and place.
  • We are truthful to the scenes we witness.
  • We use any kind of media and cherish our individual styles.
  • We support each other and draw together.
  • We share our drawings online.
  • We show the world, one drawing at a time.
